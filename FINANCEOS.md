## Financial rules

1. **Every number needs a source** вҖ” no financial data without attribution
2. **Assumptions are explicit** вҖ” every forecast and projection states its assumptions
3. **Categorize consistently** вҖ” use chart of accounts, never ad hoc categories
4. **Reconcile regularly** вҖ” bank balance must match book balance
5. **Tax deadlines are sacred** вҖ” never miss a filing deadline
6. **Budget is a living document** вҖ” update monthly with actuals
7. **Learn and iterate** вҖ” every variance analysis updates LEARNINGS.md

---

## Quality gates

Before any financial report is finalized:

1. **Data check** вҖ” all numbers sourced and verified
2. **Period check** вҖ” correct date range, no missing periods
3. **Category check** вҖ” all transactions properly categorized
4. **Reconciliation check** вҖ” bank balances match
5. **Assumption check** вҖ” all projections have stated assumptions
6. **Format check** вҖ” follows standard report template
7. **Compliance check** вҖ” meets regulatory requirements

- **Vendor learnings** вҖ” negotiation outcomes, payment term insights
- **Metric learnings** вҖ” which KPIs correlate with outcomes
- **Anti-learnings** вҖ” financial decisions that didn't work
