<objective>
Set up a new financial workspace. From chart of accounts to budget to first cash flow forecast.

Company name: $ARGUMENTS
</objective>

<process>
1. Display mode header: `<< FINANCE:OS // ONBOARD >>`
2. Role selection: Founder / CFO / Controller / Bookkeeper / Fractional CFO
3. Collaboration: Solo / Team
4. Company basics: entity type, jurisdiction, fiscal year, accounting basis, currency
5. Chart of accounts setup (defaults + customize)
6. Bank connections
7. Revenue streams, pricing, MRR/ARR
8. Major expenses, recurring costs, vendors
9. Initial budget framework
10. Tax obligations and deadlines
11. Create workspace from _template/, fill answers, display summary
</process>
