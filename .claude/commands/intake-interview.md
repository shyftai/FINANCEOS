# FINANCE:OS Intake Interview

Structured onboarding interview for finance workspaces. Ask one block at a time.

## Block 1 — Company & Stage
- Company name and industry?
- Revenue range? (pre-revenue, <$1M, $1-10M, $10M+)
- Funding stage? (bootstrapped, seed, Series A+)
- Entity type? (LLC, C-Corp, S-Corp)

Save to: `workspace.config.md`

## Block 2 — Accounting Setup
- Chart of accounts established? (standard, custom)
- Fiscal year end? (calendar, custom)
- Accounting method? (cash, accrual)

Save to: `ACCOUNTING.md`

## Block 3 — Banking
- Primary bank? (Mercury, SVB, Chase, other)
- Payment processors? (Stripe, PayPal)
- Multi-currency? (currencies used)

Save to: `BANKING.md`

## Block 4 — Revenue Model
- Revenue model? (subscription, transactional, usage-based, hybrid)
- Billing frequency? (monthly, annual, usage)
- Average contract value?

Save to: `REVENUE.md`

## Block 5 — Expense Categories
- Top expense categories? (payroll, SaaS, marketing, COGS)
- Payroll provider? (Gusto, Rippling, Deel)
- Monthly burn rate estimate?

Save to: `EXPENSES.md`

## Block 6 — Tools
- Accounting? (QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks)
- Billing? (Stripe, Chargebee, Recurly)
- Banking API? (Mercury, Plaid)
- Expense management? (Brex, Ramp, Expensify)

Save to: `TOOLS.md`

## Block 7 — Reporting
- Who receives financial reports? (board, investors, internal)
- Reporting frequency? (monthly, quarterly)
- Key metrics tracked? (ARR, burn, runway, margins)

Save to: `REPORTING.md`

## Block 8 — Budget
- Budget structure? (by department, project, cost center)
- Approval workflows? (thresholds, approvers)
- Reforecast frequency?

Save to: `BUDGET.md`

## Block 9 — Tax & Compliance
- Tax jurisdictions? (US states, international)
- Sales tax obligations? (Avalara, manual)
- Audit requirements?

Save to: `COMPLIANCE.md`

## Block 10 — Goals
- Runway target? (months)
- Margin targets? (gross, net)
- Key financial milestones?

Save to: `ROADMAP.md`

---
After all blocks: display summary and suggest `/finance:dashboard` or `/finance:report`.
