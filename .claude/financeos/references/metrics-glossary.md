# Financial Metrics Glossary

## Revenue metrics

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| MRR | Sum of monthly recurring revenue | Monthly revenue baseline |
| ARR | MRR × 12 | Annualized revenue run rate |
| Revenue growth (MoM) | (This month - Last month) / Last month | Monthly momentum |
| Net revenue retention | (Starting MRR + Expansion - Churn - Contraction) / Starting MRR | Revenue quality |
| Gross revenue retention | (Starting MRR - Churn - Contraction) / Starting MRR | Customer retention |
| ARPU | Total revenue / Total customers | Revenue per customer |

## Profitability metrics

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| Gross margin | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | Production efficiency |
| Net margin | Net income / Revenue | Overall profitability |
| Operating margin | Operating income / Revenue | Core business profitability |
| EBITDA | Net income + Interest + Tax + Depreciation + Amortization | Cash-generating ability |

## Cash metrics

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| Burn rate (gross) | Total monthly expenses | Monthly cash outflow |
| Burn rate (net) | Expenses - Revenue | Net monthly cash drain |
| Runway | Cash balance / Net burn rate | Months until cash runs out |
| Quick ratio | (Cash + AR) / Current liabilities | Short-term liquidity |
| Cash conversion cycle | DSO + DIO - DPO | Days to convert investment to cash |

## Unit economics

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| CAC | Total S&M spend / New customers | Cost to acquire a customer |
| LTV | ARPU × Gross margin × (1 / Churn rate) | Total customer value |
| LTV:CAC | LTV / CAC | Unit economics health (target >3x) |
| Payback period | CAC / (ARPU × Gross margin) | Months to recoup CAC |

## Growth metrics

| Metric | Formula | What it tells you |
|--------|---------|------------------|
| Rule of 40 | Revenue growth % + Profit margin % | Growth vs profitability balance |
| Magic number | Net new ARR / Prior quarter S&M spend | Sales efficiency |
| Burn multiple | Net burn / Net new ARR | Capital efficiency |
