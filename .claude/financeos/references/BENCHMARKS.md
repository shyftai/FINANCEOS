# Financial Benchmarks

## SaaS benchmarks

| Metric | Poor | Average | Good | Excellent |
|--------|------|---------|------|-----------|
| MoM revenue growth (early) | <5% | 5-10% | 10-20% | >20% |
| Net revenue retention | <90% | 90-100% | 100-120% | >120% |
| Gross margin | <50% | 50-70% | 70-80% | >80% |
| LTV:CAC | <1x | 1-3x | 3-5x | >5x |
| CAC payback (months) | >24 | 12-24 | 6-12 | <6 |
| Rule of 40 | <20 | 20-40 | 40-60 | >60 |
| Burn multiple | >3x | 2-3x | 1-2x | <1x |
| Magic number | <0.5 | 0.5-0.75 | 0.75-1.0 | >1.0 |

## Startup runway benchmarks

| Stage | Minimum runway | Comfortable | Ideal |
|-------|---------------|-------------|-------|
| Pre-seed | 12 months | 18 months | 24 months |
| Seed | 12 months | 18 months | 24 months |
| Series A | 18 months | 24 months | 30 months |
| Series B+ | 18 months | 24 months | 36 months |

## Expense benchmarks (% of revenue)

| Category | Early stage | Growth | Scale |
|----------|-----------|--------|-------|
| COGS | 15-30% | 15-25% | 10-20% |
| R&D / Engineering | 30-50% | 25-35% | 15-25% |
| Sales & Marketing | 30-60% | 25-45% | 20-35% |
| G&A | 15-25% | 10-20% | 8-15% |

## Cash flow benchmarks

| Metric | Concerning | Acceptable | Strong |
|--------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Days Sales Outstanding | >90 | 30-60 | <30 |
| Days Payable Outstanding | <15 | 30-45 | 45-60 |
| Operating cash flow margin | Negative | 0-10% | >10% |
