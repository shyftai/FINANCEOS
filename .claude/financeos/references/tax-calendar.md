# FINANCE:OS Tax Calendar

## Federal Tax Deadlines

### C-Corporation (Form 1120)

| Filing | Deadline | Extension | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Annual return | April 15 | October 15 (6 months) | Calendar year filers |
| Quarterly estimated tax | Apr 15, Jun 15, Sep 15, Dec 15 | None | Form 1120-W |
| Extension filing | April 15 | — | Form 7004 |

### S-Corporation (Form 1120-S)

| Filing | Deadline | Extension | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Annual return | March 15 | September 15 (6 months) | K-1s due to shareholders |
| Quarterly estimated tax | Apr 15, Jun 15, Sep 15, Jan 15 | None | Shareholder responsibility |
| Extension filing | March 15 | — | Form 7004 |

### Partnership / LLC (Form 1065)

| Filing | Deadline | Extension | Notes |
|--------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Annual return | March 15 | September 15 (6 months) | K-1s due to partners |
| Extension filing | March 15 | — | Form 7004 |

### Payroll Tax

| Filing | Deadline | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Form 941 (quarterly) | Apr 30, Jul 31, Oct 31, Jan 31 | Federal payroll tax |
| Form 940 (annual FUTA) | January 31 | Federal unemployment tax |
| W-2s to employees | January 31 | Also file with SSA |
| W-2s to SSA | January 31 | Electronic or paper |
| 1099-NEC to contractors | January 31 | Non-employee compensation |
| 1099-NEC to IRS | January 31 | Electronic filing |
| 1099-MISC to IRS | March 31 (electronic) | Other types of income |

## Sales Tax

| Frequency | When | Typical States |
|-----------|------|---------------|
| Monthly | 20th of following month | CA, NY, TX (high volume) |
| Quarterly | Last day of month following quarter | Most states (moderate volume) |
| Annual | January 31 | Low volume states |

**Economic nexus thresholds (post-Wayfair):**
Most states: $100K in sales OR 200 transactions per year.

---

## Calendar Setup Rules

1. **Set alerts at 30 and 7 days** before every deadline
2. **Extension ≠ payment extension** — estimated taxes are still due on original date
3. **State deadlines may differ** from federal — always check each state
4. **Multi-state filers:** Track nexus states and their individual deadlines
5. **Payroll deadlines are non-negotiable** — penalties are severe and personal
6. **Always use electronic filing** where available — extends some deadlines
